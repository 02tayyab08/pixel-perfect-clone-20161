## Waivers / exemptions

- Additional fee for **General Trading** activity is **waived** for new applications and the next three renewals (4 years).
- **Cross Business Activity Fee** is **waived** for new applications and the next three renewals (4 years).
- License Upgrade “Amendment Fee” is waived for qualifying new related business license applications (as stated in the September 2024 price list notes).

## Extra activities and cross activity

| Label | Figure (AED) |
|-------|----------------|
| Extra Business Activity Fee (three activities included free per Business License Package; additional up to max 7) | 1,000 per Additional Business Activity |
| Cross Business Activity Fee (combining professional and commercial business activities under one Business License) | 2,000 |

When Cross Business Activity Fee is waived (new applications + next three renewals / 4 years), the effective amount for a new application is **0**.
